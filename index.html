Rules and Regulations

1. Enrollment and registration must be completed within the specified deadlines.

2. Seat availability is limited, and seats are allocated based on factors like capacity and the number of students enrolled in each course.

3. Seat allotment can be done randomly or based on students' preferences, depending on the institution's policies.

4. Communication regarding allotted exam centers or seats will be provided within a specified timeframe.

5. Students must regularly check official communication channels for updates on seat allotment.

6. Students should be present at the designated exam center on time.

7. Students must carry the necessary identification documents as per the institution's guidelines.

8. Adherence to the exam rules and guidelines provided by the institution is mandatory.

9. Students requiring special accommodations should inform the institution in advance and provide relevant documentation.

10. Any violation of the exam rules and regulations may result in penalties or disqualification.
